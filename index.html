The movie "Road House"




Ok, so this movie is pretty out-dated, and there are DEFINITELY things from this movie that shouldn't even be discussed on this page, but this movie is hilariously awesome to me. There are some pretty well-known actors in it, which makes it even more interesting that the movie got made. Was the concept of road houses even a thing? Did they ever look as terrible or as "classy" as they did in that movie? Why is everyone ready to attack each other in the '80s? Did everyone know karate at birth in those days?



Let's chat about the main character, Dalton, played by Patrick Swayze. Is he supposed to be a "good guy"? I know he starts out with this attitude that fighting should be a last resort, but after the first 10 mins or so, that is completely out the window. So are about 12 different patrons of a particular road house, personally round-house-kicked by Dalton himself.



This starts a rockabilly-fueled battle royale that doesn't really stop until the credits roll. Like any quality '80s action movie, there is a monster truck, but this is somehow a tame part of the movie, and almost feels expected by the time it shows up. Also surprisingly, Dalton chain-smokes cigarettes (even while practicing Tai Chi). I could go on for a while, but I just want to say this movie is as confusing as it is hilarious.
